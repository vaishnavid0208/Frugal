Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the most asked questions by our users

What is a FRUGAL?
FRUGAL is a free website that compare prices of a particular product for various e-commerce stores. It also helps you to choose the product at the best price.
What is the use of FRUGAL?
E-commerce stores change price of their products very frequently. It is recommended to compare priceof products you are looking to buy.
What stores are supported FRUGAL?
FRUGAL supports major e-commerce stores from India (i.e. Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc). We are working on adding more stores and support them soon.
